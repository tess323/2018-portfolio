Work

Tess O'Brien

I am a web developer and designer located in Seattle who is passionate about working with teams to creatively solve problems. I am fascinated with where design meets development. My time in the start-ups has taught me to be scrappy and tenacious. I believe that an intuitive and intentional web presence can simplify a hectic world.

I believe in trying new things and pursuing them until they make sense. I have been working with FuseIQ for the past two years to creatively solve non-profit problems using CMS solutions such as Drupal and WordPress. When I am not exploring new languages, seeking out design trends or participating in women in tech events, I am exploring the Pacific Northwest or a new recipe. I want to continue to build and continue to learn.

YWCA

Fuse IQ partnered with YWCA to build a scalable site to manage their programs, events and news needs. I joined the project as one of two developers, focused on executing a particularly difficult design. Since launch I have taken over as lead developer on the project and just launched a major workflow initiative, leveraging Drupal's permissions to make the site usable for unlimited content editors.

FuseIQ

I acted as the lead developer on FuseIQ's brand relaunch. This Drupal 8 site, is heavily design and SEO focused. Using paragraphs, this site takes a component based approach and is extremely scalable. I worked closely with our team to translate ambitious designs into something we could build on a tight timeline that is SEO friendly.

University of Washington Department of Nuerosurgery

FuseIQ partnered with University of Washington Department of Nuerosurgery to build a Drupal solution to replace an outdated site. As the solo developer on my first D8 site I had the chance to experiement with panels and the new and improved Drupal 8 platform.

Operation Nightwatch

Operation Nightwatch came to FuseIQ to build a quick and affordable WordPress solution using a WordPress template that was then customized.

Away Home Washington

This solo project stood as a chance for me to self-teach myself Wordpress. FuseIQ partnered with Away Home WA to build a templated WordPress site with a custom gallery.

Seattle Housing Authority

One of my first Drupal 7 sites, this project served as a chance to get my feet wet with FuseIQ and the Drupal platform. This complex site is a search tool for affordable housing in Seattle.

Conway Jarvis

This simple templated WordPress site was built on an expediated timeline for FuseIQ to show their ability to rapidly develop simple sites.
